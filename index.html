About Me

My full name is Tien Thanh Ngo, but people call me Thanh. It all started in 7th grade after I migrated from Vietnam. Like most people, I play a lot of video games in my middle and high school years. But what different was that I'm a very competitive gamer, I would spent hours trying to be the top players. The games that I used to play was very time consuming so I wanted to find a way to do more for less. Then I discovered Autohotkey (a scripting language that let you manipulate keystrokes and mouse) and I start making tools to boost my gaming experience. Days by days, scripts here after scripts, and here I am !
